What You'll Experience

7-Day Programme Highlights

Day 1-2: Introduction to royal fashion history and textile analysis at Hampton Court Palace
Day 3-5: Hands-on creation of bespoke Tyvek replica garments sized to fit, guided by Kingston's Creative Practitioners
Day 6: Final garment assembly and styling workshop
Day 7: Showcase presentation and graduation ceremony
Evenings: Exclusive local experiences including storytelling events at Community Brain's FuseBox

Unique Educational Value

  • Parent & Student Co-Participation: Designed for families to experience together, building confidence for overseas study
  • AFHEA & Makerversity Accredited: Learn from Kingston's uniquely qualified Creative Practitioners
  • Historic Royal Palaces Partnership: Exclusive access to Hampton Court's collections and expertise
  • Authentic Student Living: Stay in premium Kingston student residences or certified partners like The Rex Kingston
  • Local Cultural Immersion: Evening events with Community Brain exploring Kingston's vibrant creative community

Accommodation & Location

Your stay includes premium accommodation options that give authentic insight into student life at Kingston University:

  • Kingston Student Residences: Modern, fully-equipped apartments in the heart of campus life
  • The Rex Kingston: Certified partner accommodation offering boutique comfort with local character
  • Location Benefits: Walking distance to Hampton Court Palace, River Thames, and Kingston's historic market town
  • Transport Links: 30 minutes to Central London by train, easy access to all major attractions

Why This Matters

This disruptive model represents a new way to experience higher education:

  • Preview Before Commitment: Families can assess Kingston University and UK education before making a full degree commitment
  • Cultural Confidence Building: Overcome anxiety about overseas study through supported immersion
  • Tourism Meets Education: Promoted through Visit Britain and TripAdvisor, bringing high-value "educational tourists" to Kingston
  • Community Economic Impact: Benefits local businesses, accommodation providers, and cultural organizations
  • University Recruitment Pipeline: Creates direct pathways for qualified international students
